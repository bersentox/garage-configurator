# Visual Reference Notes

Use `unified-navigation.reference.png` as the visual target.

## Important visual principles

* Clean premium layout
* Strong horizontal main axis
* Large oval stage pills in one row
* Active stage visually dominant
* Vertical child stack appears directly under active stage
* Expanded child becomes a card in place
* No tooltip clouds
* No radial geometry
* No floating detached elements
* Soft shadows, restrained depth
* Spacious layout with clear hierarchy
* Precise alignment and spacing
* Calm premium interaction style, closer to Tesla / Stripe than to infographic diagrams

## What matters most

1. Geometry and hierarchy
2. Spacing and alignment
3. Expansion behavior
4. Card feel of expanded child
5. Controlled, minimal visual language

## What not to do

* Do not recreate old unified-navigation radial style
* Do not add decorative circles/orbits/tooltips
* Do not turn this into a mind map
* Do not make it look playful or infographic-like
